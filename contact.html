FAQS

1. What is the age policy for the rock festival?

LIMP Festival is an all-ages event. However, attendees under the age of 18 must be accompanied by a legal guardian or an adult over the age of 21. Please bring valid identification, such as a driver's license or passport, to ensure smooth entry.

2. Can I bring outside food and beverages to the festival?

Outside food and beverages, including alcohol, are not allowed into the festival grounds. We offer a diverse range of food and drink vendors to satisfy your cravings. Stay hydrated and enjoy the delicious offerings from our festival vendors.

3. What items are allowed and prohibited at the festival?

We encourage attendees to bring essentials like sunscreen, hats, and comfortable clothing. However, items such as large umbrellas, glass containers, and illegal substances are strictly prohibited. Please refer to our official guidelines for a comprehensive list of allowed and restricted items.

4. Is there parking available, and what are the transportation options to the festival?

Yes, we offer parking facilities near the festival grounds. Additionally, we recommend exploring public transportation options or rideshare services to reduce traffic congestion and minimize environmental impact. Check our website for detailed information on parking locations and alternative transportation options.
